Tier 1 Interventions Menu

Curriculum & Instruction
  • Explicit phonics instruction (e.g., Heggerty, Jolly Phonics)
  • Inquiry-based modules (science, social studies)
  • Direct instruction with think-alouds
  • Reciprocal teaching
  • Concept mapping
  • Guided practice with immediate feedback
  • Gradual Release of Responsibility (GRR)
  • Cooperative learning and structured partner talk
  • Peer tutoring
  • Learning contracts
UDL: Representation
  • Captioned videos
  • Digital texts with TTS and adjustable features
  • Anchor charts and visuals
  • Graphic organisers (mind maps, Venn diagrams)
  • Dual coding (visuals + words)
  • Multi-sensory input (auditory + visual cues)
UDL: Engagement
  • Interactive tech tools (Jamboard, whiteboards)
  • Choice boards for interest-based access
  • Gamification tools (Kahoot, Blooket, Gimkit)
  • Culturally responsive provocations
  • Open-ended provocations and hooks
UDL: Expression
  • Digital storytelling tools (Storybird, Canva)
  • Speech-to-text and audio recording tools
  • Student-selected output formats (video, comics, presentations)
  • Low floor/high ceiling design
  • Flexible assessment and creative project options
Differentiation Strategies
  • Tiered assignments by readiness
  • Learning menus with scaffolds
  • Choice boards with visual support
  • Flexible grouping (needs/interests/strengths)
  • Mini-lessons and small group follow-up
  • Leveled materials and scaffolded texts
Executive Functioning Supports
  • Daily planners, visual schedules, timers
  • Digital calendars and agenda apps
  • ‘Get Ready, Do, Done’ frames
  • Step-by-step checklists and task breakdowns
  • Self-monitoring tools (trackers, reflection check-ins)
  • Visual timers and reminder cues
Social-Emotional Learning (SEL)
  • Mindfulness routines (5-4-3-2-1, breathing breaks)
  • Restorative circles and relationship check-ins
  • Emotion regulation anchor charts and tools
  • Role play and social stories
  • Perspective-taking and empathy-building activities
  • Zones of Regulation visual supports
Behaviour & Classroom Management
  • Co-created expectations and class norms
  • Visual behaviour matrices and prompts
  • Calm Down Corners or Reset Zones
  • Scheduled brain breaks and movement routines
  • Predictable routines and clear visual structure
  • Positive reinforcement systems (e.g., token economies)
Culturally Responsive & Relevant Teaching
  • Texts reflecting student identity & lived experience
  • Home language materials or dual-language books
  • Community guest speakers and co-teaching
  • Projects linked to local/global issues
  • Student choice in inquiry and content
  • Celebration of cultural holidays, identities, and voices
High-Impact Instructional Practices
  • Learning intentions & success criteria posted
  • Modelled and worked examples with scaffolding
  • Think-alouds and metacognitive prompts
  • Timely, specific, and descriptive feedback
  • Spaced and retrieval practice
  • Peer tutoring and teaching routines
Formative Assessment
  • Exit tickets and quick writes
  • Digital formative tools (Padlet, Quizizz, Formative)
  • Peer- and self-assessment rubrics
  • Learning check-ins (thumbs up/down, fist-to-five)
  • Student conferencing
  • Success criteria checklists
Family & Community Engagement
  • Weekly bilingual newsletters
  • Parent-teacher conferences with interpretation
  • Strengths-based phone/email updates
  • Homework choice boards and menus
  • Family invitations to events and community celebrations
  • Collaborative goal setting with families
